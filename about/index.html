Curriculum Vitae.

Hi there, this is Binggui Zhou

  • I’m currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) with the SKL-IOTSC and Department of ECE, University of Macau, Macao SAR, China; and also a Research Assistant with the SISSE, Jinan University, Zhuhai, China.

  • I’m co-supervised by Prof. Shaodan Ma (SMIEEE, Assistant Director of SKL-IOTSC) and Prof. Guanghua Yang (FIET, SMIEEE, Associate Dean of SISSE), and working on Artifical Intelligence towards Intelligent Wireless Communications.

  • I also collaborate closely with Prof. Xi Yang (Young Professor, East China Normal University) on Intelligent Wireless Communications.

  • Previously, I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering (EE) from Jinan University, Zhuhai, China and Master of Science in ECE from University of Macau, Macao SAR, China.
