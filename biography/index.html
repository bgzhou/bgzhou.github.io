I’m currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) with the SKL-IOTSC and Department of ECE, University of Macau, Macao SAR, China; and also a Research Assistant with the SISSE, Jinan University, Zhuhai, China. I’m co-supervised by Prof. Shaodan Ma (SMIEEE, Associate Director of SKL-IOTSC) and Prof. Guanghua Yang (FIET, SMIEEE, Associate Dean of SISSE).

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering (EE) from Jinan University, Zhuhai, China and Master of Science in ECE from University of Macau, Macao SAR, China.

My research interests include Intelligent Wireless Communications, Spatial-Temporal Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Generative Artificial Intelligence, and Smart Healthcare.

I also collaborate closely with Prof. Xi Yang (Professor, East China Normal University) on Intelligent Wireless Communications, and with Prof. Yunxuan Dong (Assistant Professor, Guangxi University) on Spatial-Temporal Data Mining.
